Lazy Susan

Jolissa can also design any lazy susan any way you want. A lazy susan is a round turntable or rotating tray that can be made out of glass, wood, or plastic. They are usually placed in the center of a table to aid in distributing food or other items. Add your own design and colors to create your lazy susan the way you imagine.
